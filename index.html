Services

Some of our services for law offices include:


  •  Paperless Solutions

    Stop dragging boxes of files to court. Interested in moving towards the goal of a paperless practice, we can recommend and deliver the tools, applications and equipment to help you.


  •  Cloud Computing

    The Law Society of BC has released guidelines for Cloud Computing. Actual location of your data storage is one of the biggest issues. Services such as Dropbox, iCloud, Google Drive are not a solution endorsed for by the LSBC.


  • Secure Data Backup

    Secure solutions for data backup, retrieval and sharing, using advanced encryption inside Canada. Your client files need to backed up locally and remotely in case of disaster.


  • Confidentiality of Client Files

    We offer privacy assessments that move beyond basic law society compliance to provide your organization with an effective approach to meet and exceed your data handling obligations.


  • Remote Management

    We offer Remote and onsite management solutions to keep your workstations up to date, secure, virus free and working smoothly. Keeping computer downtime to a minimum helps you.
